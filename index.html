Our Features

Here are some of our perks

Bulk Inward

Use single line entries or bulk uploads via excel files to inward items quickly. Add non existing items on the go & fill in the details later

Bulk Outward

Use single lines or select multiple items to send it out of inventory quickly

Stock Transfer

Move multiple or single items simply by choosing locations. Quick and easy transfers with intuitive color changing rows to highlight items affected due to transfer

Compact Dashboard

View precise details and summary on an easily accessible dashboard. Choose what you want to see & hide everything you don’t want to see.

Masters for Everything–

Complete flexibility to add multiple locations, brands, buyers, suppliers and items.

Track Every Item

Track and view everything about every Single item. From the time it is entered into the inventory to the time it is sold or returned.
